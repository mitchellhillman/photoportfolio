Hi!

I am a software engineer and amateur photographer living in Charlottesville, Virginia. When a hand injury kept me from practicing painting and drawing I bought a camera and discovered the absolute joy of photography. Given my background in oil painting and abiding interest in art history I am particularly inspired by the composition of the great masters. But, I'm also keenly interested in capturing honest images which witness not only to Beauty but also Truth. This means I do not edit my photos heavily and prefer portraits with natural expressions.

Thanks for your interest.

— Mitch
